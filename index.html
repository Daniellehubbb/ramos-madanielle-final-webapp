What is CashMate?

CashMate is a web-based expense tracker designed to make financial management easier for everyone. Whether you’re a student, working individual, small business owner, or part of a household, CashMate allows you to record your transactions, monitor your spending habits, and view insightful reports anytime, anywhere.
